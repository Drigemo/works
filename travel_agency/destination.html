Visit Amsterdam

Amsterdam is the capital city of the Netherlands, known for its artistic heritage, elaborate canal system, and narrow houses with gabled facades, legacies of the city’s 17th-century Golden Age.

Its Museum District houses the Van Gogh Museum, works by Rembrandt and Vermeer at the Rijksmuseum, and modern art at the Stedelijk. Cycling is key to the city’s character, and there are numerous bike paths.

Amsterdam is also famous for its vibrant nightlife, diverse culture, and rich history. Whether you're exploring the Anne Frank House or taking a boat tour through the canals, there's something for everyone in this beautiful city.
